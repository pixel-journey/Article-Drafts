# Image & Graphics Prompts for "What is the Pixel Journey?" Article

**Style Guidelines (for all visuals):**
- Pixel art / retro pixel adventure aesthetic mixed with clean modern Web3 design
- Vibrant but harmonious color palette (think neon + earth tones + pixel classic vibes)
- Include subtle PXJ / journey branding elements (small logo, pixel trail, chain links)
- High detail, clean lines, suitable for X posts (square or 16:9), carousels, and Medium headers
- Avoid clutter; focus on storytelling and emotion (wonder, adventure, community, achievement)

## 1. Hero / Featured Image Prompt (Main Visual)
"Epic wide pixel-art illustration of a lone pixel adventurer standing at the edge of a glowing digital map, looking out over multiple blockchain chains represented as floating islands or portals (WAX crystal, Polygon purple, Base blue, ETH silver). In the foreground: a stylized Pixal PFP character with journey gear. Background shows subtle PxLanding hub, mini-game elements (tower defense silhouettes), and a bright path of pixels leading forward. Retro pixel game style meets modern vibrant Web3 illustration. Bright optimistic lighting, sense of wonder and invitation. Text overlay space at top or bottom for title. 16:9 landscape, high resolution."

## 2. Carousel / Infographic Slide Ideas (5-6 slides)

**Slide 1 (Hook):** Same as hero but with title "What is the Pixel Journey?" and subtitle "Your pixel-perfect Web3 adventure starts here"

**Slide 2 (Core Idea):** Split screen - left: simple pixel character discovering a map; right: icons for Education, Creation, Economy + short text bullets.

**Slide 3 (Progression Path):** Visual timeline or winding pixel road with 12 steps as glowing milestones or checkpoints. Small icons for each major phase.

**Slide 4 (PxPortals Vision):** Central glowing hub (PxLanding) with connected portals as orbiting or linked buildings: PxLearn (book icon), PxMarket (shop), PxStaking (chart), PxHot (arena), etc. Multi-chain bridges as glowing links.

**Slide 5 (Community & Future):** Diverse group of pixel characters (different styles, some with tools) standing together looking at a bright horizon with mini-game silhouettes and DAO symbols. Text: "Built together. Owned together. Journeying together."

**Slide 6 (CTA):** Clean pixel-art style with big "Start Your Journey" button visual + links/icons for Discord, X, Docs, Stake portal. Bright celebratory feel.

## 3. Fun / Meme-Style Visual Prompts
- "Pixel Journey vs Regular Web3 Project" meme format: left sad/confused degen in boring office, right happy pixel adventurer with map and friends in colorful world.
- Simple "5-Minute Quest" comic strip style (4-6 panels) showing quick onboarding wins.
- "The 12 Steps" as a literal video game level select screen with pixel character choosing path.
