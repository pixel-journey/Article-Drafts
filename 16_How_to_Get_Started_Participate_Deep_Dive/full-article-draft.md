# How to Get Started & Participate in the Pixel Journey

**Your On-Ramp to the Adventure**

Whether you’re completely new to the Pixel Journey or looking to deepen your participation, this guide provides clear, actionable steps to get involved meaningfully. The ecosystem is designed with multiple on-ramps, so there’s a natural path for almost everyone.

This is the definitive practical guide to getting started and participating in the Pixel Journey.

## 1. Quick Start Checklist

1. **Understand the basics** — Read the high-level overview (Article 01) and Origin Story (Article 03).
2. **Join the community** — Discord is the main hub for discussion, support, and updates.
3. **Acquire a Pixal (or start with other assets)** — The genesis Pixal PFPs are the most direct entry point.
4. **Set up a WAX wallet** (if you don’t have one) and learn basic bridging if you want to explore multi-chain.
5. **Start small and learn by doing** — Stake, craft, or bridge a little to get comfortable.

## 2. Acquiring Your First Assets

### Option A: Buy a Pixal PFP (Recommended for Most New Users)
- Primary marketplace: NFTHive or AtomicHub on WAX
- Look for good entry-level Pixals or specific traits you like
- Consider budget, rarity, and long-term utility (e.g., staking power, future gameplay)

### Option B: Start with Other Ecosystem Assets
- Some participants begin by acquiring $KING, WPIXAL, or ingredients through secondary markets or farming
- This can be a lower-cost way to get exposure while learning

### Option C: Bridge In (For Multi-Chain Users)
- If you already hold assets on Polygon, Base, Ethereum, or BNB Chain, you can bridge into the Pixel Journey ecosystem
- Useful for those who want to explore cross-chain opportunities early

## 3. Core Participation Activities

### Staking (Easiest Ongoing Participation)
- Go to stake.pixeljourney.xyz
- Stake your Pixals and/or ingredients for steady PXJ + WUF rewards
- Longer lockups and consistent participation can earn leaderboard recognition and bonus rewards
- Non-custodial — assets stay in your wallet

### Crafting & Recycling
- Use the crafting system to turn Pixals into Pixel Ingredient Packs
- Upgrade ingredients through tiers for higher staking yields and future utility
- Contributes to overall ecosystem health (supply reduction)

### Governance Participation
- Hold a WaxRock for direct voting power in the WaxRocks Council
- Build Discord levels and stake consistently to increase influence
- Stay informed and contribute ideas that can become council motions
- Real decisions are made around treasury, incentives, and ecosystem direction

### Bridging & Multi-Chain Exploration
- Bridge Pixals or other assets to Polygon, Base, Ethereum, or BNB Chain
- Access new DeFi opportunities and position yourself for future cross-chain features
- Use official bridges and follow security best practices

### Education & Skill Building
- Use existing docs and community resources to learn
- Prepare for PxLearn Web3 Academy when it launches
- Hands-on activities (staking, crafting, bridging) are the best teachers

## 4. Preparing for Future Features

- Accumulate and upgrade assets (Pixals, ingredients) for future gameplay utility
- Participate in current systems to build progression/achievements that may carry forward via PxPackages
- Stay engaged with governance so you have a voice in how new features roll out
- Build knowledge through education resources so you’re ready when Px-dApps and mini-games launch

## 5. Security & Best Practices

- Use official links and verified contracts only
- Never share seed phrases or private keys
- Start small when trying new mechanics (staking, bridging, crafting)
- Join Discord for real-time support and verified information
- Be cautious of scams and impersonators (common in Web3)

## 6. Finding Your Place

There is no single "correct" way to participate. Some people focus on collecting and long-term holding. Others enjoy active crafting, governance, or helping new members. Many do a combination.

The Pixel Journey rewards consistent, thoughtful participation over time. Find the activities that resonate with you and build from there.

## Conclusion: Start Where You Are

Getting started in the Pixel Journey is straightforward, but the depth comes from consistent participation over time. Whether you begin with a single Pixal, some staking, or simply joining the Discord and learning, every step counts.

The ecosystem is designed to grow with you. The more you engage thoughtfully, the more value and belonging you’ll find.

**The pixels are waiting. Your journey starts now.**
