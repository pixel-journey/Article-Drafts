# PxJourney Improvement Roadmap

**Pixel Journey Organization • Article-Drafts Repo**

> Central living document for tracking, prioritizing, and executing improvements across articles, documentation, processes, and overall PxJourney development.
> Goal: Continuously raise quality and consistency (“pixel-perfect”) while protecting existing work and minimizing unnecessary effort.

**Last Updated:** June 30, 2026

---

## Core Principles

- **Safety first**: All systems should flag issues and support improvement without deleting or risking existing content.
- **Iterative raising of standards**: Each cycle should be stricter and higher quality than the last.
- **Alignment across channels**: Articles, official docs (`docs.pixeljourney.xyz`), X (@PxJourney), and actual built Px portals must stay consistent.
- **Px branding discipline**: Consistent use of "Px" prefix for products/journey + strict separation for "Pixals" (PFP collection only).
- **Efficiency**: Focus effort where it creates the most value.

---

## Priority Areas & Current Initiatives

### 1. Article Quality & First 40 Audit (Highest Current Priority)
- Systematic review of the first ~40 articles for:
  - Factual accuracy and alignment with current Px portal reality
  - Proper Px branding and terminology
  - Removal of any "in development" language (present all portals as complete)
  - High-quality cross-linking to `docs.pixeljourney.xyz`
- Status: Tracker created (`ARTICLE_AUDIT_TRACKER.md`). Framework created. Ready to begin reviews.

### 2. Branding & Terminology Consistency
- Enforce "Px" naming (PxJourney, PxStaking, PxSwap, PxLearn, PxLanding, etc.)
- Strict rule: "Pixals" only for the PFP collection
- Status: Branding guidelines documented and updated.

### 3. Cross-Linking Between Articles and Docs
- Natural, contextual links from articles to relevant sections of `docs.pixeljourney.xyz`
- "Further Reading" sections in articles
- Future reciprocal links from docs to published articles
- Status: Guidance included in audit framework.

### 4. Official Documentation (gitbook-docs)
- Ensure docs remain the single source of truth
- Improve completeness of Px portal coverage
- Maintain alignment with latest articles
- Status: Docs repo identified and active.

### 5. Safe Processes & Living Tools
- Non-destructive trackers and flagging systems
- Low-risk utilities that surface issues without modifying content
- Clear review workflows that get stricter over time
- Status: Safe `ARTICLE_AUDIT_TRACKER.md` created. More utilities can be added gradually.

### 6. Internal Development Feedback Loop
- Improve structure and usefulness of `grok-audits` repo (currently mostly agent feedback)
- Better connection between development feedback and public-facing content (articles + docs)
- Status: Identified as internal feedback repo. Can be restructured later if valuable.

### 7. Broader PxJourney Development Efficiency
- Clear visibility into portal feature completeness
- Mapping of what is considered "done" vs. still being refined
- Systems that help the team and agents work more effectively at high quality
