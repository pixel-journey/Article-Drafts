# PixelDAO Governance & The WaxRocks Council: The Complete Deep-Dive Guide

**From Meritocratic Council to Full Community DAO**

The WaxRocks Council represents the first major step toward decentralized governance in the Pixel Journey. It is a meritocratic system that gives real power to dedicated holders while laying the foundation for a true PixelDAO.

This is the definitive deep dive into PixelDAO governance — how the council works today, who can participate, what it controls, recent activity, and the long-term vision for full community ownership.

## 1. What is the WaxRocks Council?

The WaxRocks Council is seeded by the ultra-rare WaxRock trait (only 95 exist). It serves as the practical foundation of PixelDAO, giving holders of these rare assets (and those who earn voting power through other means) direct influence over key ecosystem decisions.

It is meritocratic and additive: voting power comes from a combination of:
- WaxRock ownership (base +64 votes)
- Discord levels and special roles
- Staking volume and lockup history
- Other community contributions

## 2. What the Council Controls

The WaxRocks Council directly governs important parts of the ecosystem, including:
- Allocation of 25% of creator-market royalties to buy back $PXJ
- Distribution of portions of the monthly dao.pxj treasury (sent to council.pxj for allocation)
- Targeted DeFi boosts (e.g., 2M PXJ every two weeks to nominated pairs, 5M PXJ to LSWAX pools)
- Collaboration proposals with third-party projects
- Recurring WAX DeFi liquidity incentives

## 3. How Voting Works

Voting power is transparent and stackable. WaxRock holders start with significant base power that can be amplified through active participation (staking, Discord engagement, etc.).

Motions are proposed, discussed, and voted on within the council structure, with results implemented by the team where appropriate. This creates a bridge between community input and executable decisions.

## 4. Recent Activity & Impact

The council has already approved meaningful actions, including:
- Multiple rounds of DeFi liquidity incentives
- Collaborations with ecosystem projects
- Strategic allocation of treasury resources

This demonstrates real, functional governance rather than symbolic voting.

## 5. The Path to Full PixelDAO

The WaxRocks Council is explicitly designed as the first phase toward a broader PixelDAO. As the ecosystem matures, governance is expected to expand with:
- More inclusive voting mechanisms
- Greater treasury control by the community
- Integration with future Px-dApp features and gameplay
- Potential on-chain elements as the technical stack evolves

The goal is a mature, self-sustaining DAO that reflects the values of education, participation, and long-term building that have defined the Pixel Journey from the start.

## 6. How to Participate

- Acquire a WaxRock Pixal (highest direct voting power)
- Build Discord levels and special roles through active engagement
- Stake consistently to increase lockup-based voting weight
- Stay informed through council discussions and proposals
- Contribute ideas and feedback that can become council motions

## 7. Current State & Future Outlook

PixelDAO governance is already functional and impactful. As more Px-dApps launch and the community grows, the council (and eventual full DAO) will play an increasingly central role in shaping the direction of the Pixel Journey.

## Conclusion: Real Governance, Built to Last

The WaxRocks Council is not just a symbolic body — it is a working governance system that already influences treasury, DeFi incentives, and ecosystem strategy. It represents a thoughtful, phased approach to decentralization that prioritizes merit, participation, and long-term alignment with the project’s core values.

This is how PixelDAO begins — with real power in the hands of those who have earned it, and a clear path toward broader community ownership.

**The council is listening. The pixels have a voice.**
