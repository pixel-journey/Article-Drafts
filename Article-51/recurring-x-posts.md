## Usage Guidelines
- Post slowly and with presence. These are not filler — they are ongoing invitations to co-tend the rhythm.

- Celebrate replies that share real experiments or seasonal awareness — this reinforces the culture we’re building.

These posts help the collective remember that sustainability is not an afterthought — it is a core practice.
