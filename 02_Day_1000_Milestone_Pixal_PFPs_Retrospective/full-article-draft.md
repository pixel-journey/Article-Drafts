The Pixal PFPs are the heart of everything. What began with 10,000 Pixals on WAX is evolving exactly as planned:

- **Long-term goal**: Shrink supply back down to around 2,000 circulating Pixals through burns and recycling.
- **Progress so far**: Over 2,500 Pixals have already been forever burned in the first few years. Scarcity is tightening.
- **Current state**: Pixal PFP floors haven’t quite crossed 1,000 WAX yet, but with the broader market and our own momentum, any dip is a potential gift for new journeyers. In a year or two — or another 1,000 days from now — 5k or even 10k WAX floors aren’t out of the question for those who hold strong.

There is no “bad Pixal.” Every one fulfills its destiny — whether held for utility, airdrops, staking power, or recycled into powerful ingredients for future crafts and collectibles.
