```
#PixelJourney #PXJ [2-3 more targeted hashtags]

```

**Thread Version Add-ons:**
- Tweet 2: Key takeaway #1 + emoji
- Tweet 3: Key takeaway #2 or fun fact
- Tweet 4: How it connects to bigger vision / PxPortals / future
- Final Tweet: Strong CTA ("Tag a friend who needs to see this", "What's your favorite part of the Journey so far? Reply below", bookmark, RT to spread the pixel love)

## Best Practices for X (and cross-posting)

- Keep individual tweets under 280 chars (ideally 200-250 for readability)
- Use line breaks, bold/italic via Unicode or caps for emphasis
- Always end with a question or clear CTA to boost engagement
- Vary posting times; experiment with analytics
- Pair text posts with strong visuals (use prompts from article folders)
- For recurring content, rotate through different angles so followers don't see repeats too often
- Track performance of initial vs recurring posts to refine

## Cross-Platform Notes
- **Discord:** Longer, more detailed versions or excerpts in #education or #announcements. Use threads there too.
- **Medium:** Full article with nice formatting, cover image from prompts.
- **Future PxLearn / Website:** Embed or link articles as learning modules or blog.
