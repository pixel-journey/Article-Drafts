# Pixel Journey Article Brainstorm: 100-150+ Concepts

**Goal:** Build a comprehensive content library covering **every key aspect** of the Pixel Journey at high level first (Phase 1: ~30 articles), then spotlight/deep-dive articles (Phase 2), followed by niche, series, and ecosystem opportunity pieces (Phase 3) to reach 100-150 initial ideas. Many more will emerge as new portals, mini-games, seasons, and features launch.

This list draws from:
- Official docs (docs.pixeljourney.xyz & gitbook-docs repo)
- PxLabs vision, PxPortals & PxPackages
- Season 1 mechanics, economy, community features
- Education guides across ecosystems
- Journey use-cases for different personas
- Future plans (multi-chain, mini-games, DAO, PixelNet)
- Achievements, milestones, fun/educational angles for recurring social content

**Phases & Prioritization:**
- **Phase 1 (High-Level Core Series, folders 01-30):** Broad, accessible overviews introducing every major pillar. These are the "flagship" articles that set the foundation. Publish in sequence or as a series.
- **Phase 2 (Spotlights, Deep Dives & Personas, 31-80):** Specific features, mechanics, how the ecosystem works in practice, persona-targeted articles ("Pixel Journey for..."), achievement celebrations, mini-game & portal intros.
- **Phase 3 (Advanced, Niche, Series & Expansions, 81-150+):** Comparisons, future speculation, detailed ecosystem opportunity explorations ("How Pixel Artists can thrive on WAX/EVM/Solana with PXJ"), recurring content templates turned articles, community stories, technical deep dives, lore/world-building.

**Folder Naming Convention:** `NN_Short-Slug-Descriptive` (use kebab-case, start with number for sort order). Example: `01_What-is-the-Pixel-Journey`
