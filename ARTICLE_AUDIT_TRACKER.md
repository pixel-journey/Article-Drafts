# Article Audit Tracker (First 40+ Articles)

**Pixel Journey Organization • Article-Drafts Repo**

> **Purpose**: Track reviews and refinements of articles. This is a **safe, read-only style tracker**.
> It only flags issues and records status. It does **not** delete or modify any article content.

**Last Updated:** June 30, 2026

## How to Use

1. Add a new row when you start reviewing an article.
2. Fill in the columns as you go.
3. Update status and notes after each pass.
4. Move status to "Done (Refined)" when the article meets current standards.
